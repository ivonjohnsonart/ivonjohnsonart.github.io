About the Artist

Ivon Johnson creates oil paintings that explore the relationships between place, memory, and belonging. Through light, color, and emotion, his work captures the quiet beauty of moments and spaces that bring people together. Working from his home studio in Mountain Home, Idaho, Ivon connects with viewers through shared experiences and travel-inspired scenes.
