About

Ivon Johnson is an oil painter based in Mountain Home, Idaho. A lifelong artist and retired member of the U.S. Air Force, his work reflects his travels, experiences, and a deep appreciation for the connection between people and place.

Through the interplay of light and color, he captures the sense of memory and belonging found in the landscapes that shape our lives. His work is currently on exhibit at Sage Lodge in Pray, Montana.
