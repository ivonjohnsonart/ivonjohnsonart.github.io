About

Ivon Johnson is an artist based in Mountain Home, Idaho. A retired U.S. Air Force member, his art reflects a lifetime of travel and experience, celebrating the deep connection between people and place.

Through light and color, his work evokes memory and belonging in the landscapes that shape our lives. A selection of his work is currently on display at Sage Lodge in Pray, Montana.
