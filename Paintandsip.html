Sharing the Joy of Art, One Sip at a Time

Beyond creating fine art, Ivon loves helping others discover their own creative spark. These Paint & Sip events are his way of opening the studio door a little wider—inviting anyone, from total beginners to seasoned doodlers, to experience the fun of making something by hand.

Sip. Paint. Create Something Uniquely Yours.

Each event is relaxed, welcoming, and designed for pure enjoyment. All materials are provided, and Ivon offers gentle guidance along the way—sharing color theory, technique tips, and encouragement whenever you want it. You’ll learn just enough to feel confident, but the final masterpiece is always your own.

Your first drink is included, with both alcoholic and non-alcoholic options available. Come unwind, try something new, and enjoy an evening filled with creativity, laughter, and good company.
