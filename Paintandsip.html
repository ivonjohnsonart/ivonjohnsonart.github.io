Paint. Sip. Create Something Uniquely Yours.

Beyond creating fine art, Ivon loves bringing people together to connect, relax, and create. Paint & Sip events let Ivon open his studio door a little wider—inviting everyone to share in his love of art and connect through the simple joy of painting.

Each event brings people together in a relaxed, welcoming setting, and all the materials are provided. Ivon leads the painting from start to finish—sharing techniques, color theory, and helping you bring your own ideas to life. By the end of the evening, you’ll head home with your own personal masterpiece and a memorable creative experience.

Your first drink is included, with both alcoholic and non-alcoholic options available. Come unwind, try something new, and enjoy an evening filled with creativity, laughter, and good company.
